Steam Engine Model

2023, Thermodynamics Project

Thermodynamics Mechanical Design 3D Printing

This was my father's old Meccano steam engine from 1978! However, it needed some TLC before I could run it.

The boiler of the steam engine had broken off and needed to be riveted back in place, while the steam pipe had to be soldered. The piston was completely stuck. On the aesthetic side, the chrome boiler casing was rusted along with several other parts â€” the engine was completely non-functional.

After a full restoration, including mechanical repairs and cosmetic touch-ups, the engine now runs like a beaute!

This engine was actually the original Meccano steam engine or MEC1. However, after it was discontinued, MAMOD slightly modified it and sold it under the name SP3.

Additional note: The fuel tray in this video is not original. This engine was bought off the shelf and came without it. My father had to make his own fuel tray.
