Revimo - Robotic Mobility Device Development

During my internship at Revimo, I contributed to the development of an innovative robotic mobility device designed to assist individuals with mobility challenges. The project focused on creating a sophisticated robotic system that could provide enhanced mobility support through advanced sensor integration and intelligent control systems.

My primary responsibilities included developing and testing control algorithms for the robotic mobility device, working with embedded systems programming, and integrating various sensors to ensure safe and reliable operation. I collaborated closely with the engineering team to optimize the device's performance and user experience.

The robotic mobility device incorporated cutting-edge technologies including computer vision, sensor fusion, and adaptive control systems. I was involved in the testing and validation phases, ensuring that the device met safety standards and performance requirements for real-world deployment.

This internship provided valuable experience in robotics, embedded systems development, and working on assistive technology solutions that have the potential to significantly improve quality of life for individuals with mobility limitations.

Key Technologies

  • Embedded Systems Programming
  • Robotic Control Algorithms
  • Sensor Integration
  • Computer Vision
  • Safety Systems Design
  • User Interface Development
